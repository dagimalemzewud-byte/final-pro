About Ferrari

The Legend of Speed, Innovation, and Italian Excellence

Company Overview

Ferrari is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer known worldwide for high-performance vehicles, cutting-edge engineering, and a deep connection to motorsport. The company represents speed, prestige, and uncompromising design.
Ferrari is renowned for designing and engineering some of the world’s most iconic sports cars. Every vehicle combines cutting-edge technology, precision engineering, and aerodynamic design to deliver extreme performance and handling. From powerful V8 and V12 engines to advanced hybrid systems, Ferrari’s products reflect decades of racing expertise and innovation, ensuring unmatched driving excitement and luxury.

Founder & Inventor

Ferrari was founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939 in Maranello, Italy. Enzo Ferrari was not only an entrepreneur but also a passionate racing driver and visionary engineer. His goal was to build cars that combined racing performance with refined craftsmanship.

History & First Ferrari Car

Although the company began in 1939, the first car to officially carry the Ferrari name was the Ferrari 125 S, produced in 1947. It was powered by a V12 engine designed by Gioachino Colombo, setting the foundation for Ferrari’s engineering philosophy.

Racing Heritage

Ferrari is the most successful team in Formula One history. The brand has competed in Formula One since 1950 and remains the only team to have participated in every season of the championship.
